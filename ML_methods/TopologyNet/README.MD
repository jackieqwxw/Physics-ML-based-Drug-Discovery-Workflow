## TopologyNet

TopologyNet is a deep learning model for predicting protein–ligand binding affinity that uses topological descriptors derived from persistent homology. Instead of relying only on traditional geometric or physicochemical features, the method captures structural patterns of protein–ligand complexes through topological representations that summarize the spatial organization of atoms.

In this study, TopologyNet was included as a representative topology-based deep learning approach for binding affinity prediction. The model was applied to the prepared protein–ligand complexes in order to evaluate its ability to estimate binding free energy and distinguish active ligands from decoys within virtual screening datasets.
